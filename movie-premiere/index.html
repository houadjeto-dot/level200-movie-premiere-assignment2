SHELL OF A WOMAN

Is a life worth living if it's only lived for others?

17th AUGUST 2026

About the Movie

Marriage. Chidren. Success. Eve, is praised for doing everything "right" in life.
At a lively family celebration she smiles, plays her part, and holds her husband’s hand, yet something about her feels distant.

Beneath all the admiration, laughter and warmth, there is a quiet heaviness she cannot shake.
Early experiences of curiosity and connection are quickly met with fear and discipline, teaching her which parts of herself must remain hidden.

As Eve stands suspended between conformity and selfhood, questioning how much of herself she has sacrificed to belong,
the question lingers: how long can someone survive living as only a shell of who they truly are?

Cast

  • Eve: The protagonist. A woman who appears accomplished, respected, and fulfilled, yet is emotionally distant and internally conflicted.
  • Kwame - Eve's Husband: Eve’s partner and the embodiment of the life she was encouraged to choose.
  • Emilia - Eve's Mother: A guiding force representing societal and cultural expectations.
  • Emy - Eve's Friend: A fleeting yet pivotal presence from Eve’s childhood.
